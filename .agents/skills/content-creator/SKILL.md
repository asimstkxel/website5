# Content Creator

## Use this skill when

- Writing blog posts, articles, or long-form web content
- Creating SEO-optimized pages with proper meta tags, headings, and structure
- Writing web copy that needs to balance readability with search performance
- Generating content briefs or outlines for web pages
- Optimizing existing content for better search rankings

## Do not use this skill when

- The task is purely about visual design or layout (route to frontend-design)
- The task is about backend logic or data (route to backend-development)
- The task is about testing content (route to qa)

## SEO Content Principles

### On-Page SEO Structure

Every piece of content must follow this hierarchy:

1. **Title tag** (`<title>`): Primary keyword near the front, under 60 characters, compelling to click
2. **Meta description** (`<meta name="description">`): 150-160 characters, includes primary keyword, has a clear call to action or value proposition
3. **URL slug**: Short, lowercase, hyphenated, includes primary keyword (e.g., `first-time-buyer-mistakes`)
4. **H1**: One per page, matches search intent, contains primary keyword naturally
5. **H2s**: Break content into scannable sections, each targeting a related keyword or subtopic
6. **H3s**: Used within H2 sections for detailed breakdowns

### Keyword Strategy

- **Primary keyword**: The main search term the page targets. Use in title, H1, first paragraph, and naturally throughout (2-3% density, never forced).
- **Secondary keywords**: 3-5 related terms. Distribute across H2 headings and body paragraphs.
- **Long-tail phrases**: Natural questions or phrases people search. Use in H2/H3 headings or FAQ sections.
- **Semantic keywords**: Related concepts that show topical authority. Weave into body copy naturally.

### Content Quality Guidelines

- **Lead with value**: The first paragraph must answer the reader's core question or hook them with a specific, concrete insight. No throat-clearing.
- **Write for humans first**: SEO structure serves the reader. If a keyword doesn't fit naturally, don't force it.
- **Use specific data**: Numbers, stats, examples, and real scenarios beat vague claims. "Save up to $15,000" beats "save money."
- **One idea per paragraph**: Keep paragraphs to 2-4 sentences. Web readers scan.
- **Active voice**: "Buyers often skip inspections" not "Inspections are often skipped by buyers."
- **Internal linking**: Link to other relevant pages on the site where natural.
- **External credibility**: Reference authoritative sources when citing data or claims.

### Content Structure Template

```
Title Tag: [Primary keyword] — [Benefit or hook] | [Brand]
Meta Description: [Value proposition with primary keyword. Call to action. Under 160 chars.]

# H1: [Primary keyword in a compelling headline]

[Opening paragraph — hook + what the reader will learn + why it matters. Include primary keyword naturally.]

## H2: [Section 1 — Secondary keyword or subtopic]
[2-4 paragraphs with supporting detail, examples, data]

## H2: [Section 2 — Secondary keyword or subtopic]
[2-4 paragraphs]

## H2: [Section 3 — Secondary keyword or subtopic]
[2-4 paragraphs]

[Continue sections as needed]

## H2: Key Takeaways / What to Do Next
[Summary of actionable points. Internal link to relevant service or contact page.]

[CTA — clear next step for the reader]
```

### Blog Post Specifications

- **Word count**: 1,200-2,000 words for standard posts. Enough depth to be useful, not padded.
- **Reading level**: Grade 8-10. Clear, accessible language. Avoid jargon unless the audience expects it.
- **Tone**: Authoritative but approachable. The reader should feel guided, not lectured.
- **Images**: Suggest relevant image placements with descriptive alt text containing keywords where natural.
- **Internal links**: At least 2-3 links to other pages on the site.
- **CTA**: Every post ends with a clear call to action relevant to the content.

### Technical SEO Checklist

- [ ] Title tag under 60 characters with primary keyword
- [ ] Meta description under 160 characters with primary keyword
- [ ] One H1 per page containing primary keyword
- [ ] H2/H3 hierarchy is logical (no skipped levels)
- [ ] Images have descriptive alt text
- [ ] Internal links to relevant site pages
- [ ] URL slug is clean and keyword-rich
- [ ] Content is original and provides genuine value
- [ ] Schema markup where applicable (Article, BlogPosting, FAQ)
- [ ] Open Graph tags for social sharing
